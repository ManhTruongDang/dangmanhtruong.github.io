Every year, a minimum of 2 million people visit the GBR on commercial tours,
and many more use the Reef with their own boats and enjoy the islands and beaches.
Many visitors share their experiences via social media and this information can be
used to understand better what people do, how they engage with the Reef, and also
their perceptions of the Reef are positive or negative.
